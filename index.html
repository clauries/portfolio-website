About

Myself

Front-End Developer with a background in emotional intelligence and psychology. Recently certified as a Full Stack Web Developer from the University of Oregon’s Coding Bootcamp. The program honed my skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and responsive web design. A natural desire for order, efficiency, and accuracy is evident through a history of working in detail-oriented environments. Multi-passionate, studious explorer of information, not only seeks opportunities for learning and application of knowledge. Excited to apply an understanding of psychology to create more effective applications and interfaces for users – ultimately bridging emotional intelligence and tech development. With an Bachelors of Science degree in counseling, I have a passion for helping individuals, families, and teams utilize developmental tools for growth. Technology already plays a crucial role in our lives, providing countless opportunities for progress right in the palms of people's hands. My dream is to partner with companies and organizations to create better tools for personal and team development.
